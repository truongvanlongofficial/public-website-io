Privacy Policy

Number Reactor - Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Number Reactor ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you install, open, and play the App. The Android package name is com.longtv.numberreactor.

Summary

  • The App does not require an account.
  • The App stores gameplay progress and settings locally on your device.
  • The App uses Google AdMob for ads, including optional rewarded ads.
  • The App uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for app quality, diagnostics, and crash reporting.
  • The App uses Google's User Messaging Platform where privacy or advertising consent choices are required.
  • The App is not directed to children under 13.

Information We Collect

The App does not ask you to create an account and does not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, precise location, or payment information.

The App may process the following information:

  • Local gameplay data: score, best score, board state, run type, coins, rewards, achievements, power-up inventory, accessibility settings, sound settings, and similar progress data stored on your device.
  • Advertising data: information processed by Google AdMob to show ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and provide rewarded ads.
  • Analytics data: app usage events, app version, device information, approximate region, session activity, and similar diagnostics collected through Firebase Analytics after consent where required.
  • Crash and diagnostics data: crash reports, stack traces, app state, operating system version, device state, app version, and related diagnostics collected through Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Consent choices: privacy and advertising consent choices handled through Google's User Messaging Platform where required.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • save your local game progress and preferences;
  • operate game features such as rewards, power-ups, settings, and local statistics;
  • show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads;
  • grant in-game rewards after eligible rewarded ads are completed;
  • measure app performance and improve gameplay quality;
  • detect, diagnose, and fix crashes or technical problems;
  • comply with advertising consent, privacy, and platform requirements.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, ad interactions, coarse location signals, and diagnostic information to provide, personalize, measure, and improve ads, depending on your region and consent choices.

Rewarded ads are optional. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, the App grants the displayed in-game reward after the ad reward is confirmed.

If you publish the App on Google Play as not directed to children, make sure the target audience and content rating settings in Google Play Console match that choice. If you choose to include children in the target audience, ads and monetization must comply with Google Play Families requirements.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps and sites that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. You can also review Google's advertising information here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and Firebase Crashlytics to identify crashes and stability issues. These services are provided by Google Firebase.

Analytics collection is disabled by default in the App code and is enabled only after the App can request ads/consent as implemented in the App. Crashlytics may process crash and diagnostic information to help us fix stability issues.

You can learn more about Firebase privacy and security here: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Consent Choices in the EEA, UK, Switzerland, and Other Regions

Where required, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform to request and manage consent for ads and analytics-related processing. Depending on your location and the choices available to you, you may be able to accept, reject, or customize certain data processing purposes.

The App includes a privacy options entry in Settings when privacy options are available, so you can revisit your choices.

Google provides more information about User Messaging Platform here: https://developers.google.com/admob/android/privacy.

Local Game Data

Scores, coins, rewards, board state, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not currently provide account login, cloud sync, multiplayer servers, or online leaderboards.

You can reset progress in the App settings where available. You can also clear App data or uninstall the App through your device settings. If you uninstall the App, local game data may be deleted by your device operating system.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may allow processing of information by service providers used by the App, including Google AdMob and Firebase, as described in this Privacy Policy. These providers process information according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the developer contact information shown on the Google Play listing so we can review and take appropriate action.

Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you reset progress, clear app data, or uninstall the App. Analytics, advertising, and crash data are retained by Google according to the applicable Google and Firebase retention practices and your consent choices where applicable.

Your Choices

  • You can reset or clear local game data using the App settings or your device settings.
  • You can manage advertising choices through your device advertising settings.
  • Where available, you can revisit consent choices through the App's privacy options.
  • You can stop App data processing by uninstalling the App.
  • You may contact the developer through the Google Play listing to ask privacy questions or request assistance.

Security

We use platform and service-provider safeguards intended to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

International Processing

Google AdMob and Firebase may process information on servers located in different countries. Their processing is governed by Google's applicable terms, safeguards, and privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update it, we will change the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after an update means the revised policy applies.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer using the contact email shown on the App's Google Play listing.
