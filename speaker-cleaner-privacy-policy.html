Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026
App: Speaker Cleaner Pro
Publisher: longtv

This Privacy Policy explains how longtv ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Speaker Cleaner Pro mobile application (the "App").

Important summary: the core speaker cleaning feature works locally on your device. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not operate our own server to collect your personal data for the App's core functionality. However, the App uses Google AdMob and related Google services for ads and consent management, and those services may process certain device and advertising data as described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information processed locally on your device

To provide core features, the App may store limited information locally on your device using on-device storage such as SharedPreferences. This may include:

  • your selected cleaning settings, such as duration, intensity, mode, and cycles,
  • local run history, such as run time, selected mode, duration, cycles, and intensity,
  • local reminder preferences, such as whether reminders are enabled and the selected reminder time.

This local data is used only to make the App function properly on your device. We do not upload this local history or reminder data to our own servers.

1.2 Information collected by third-party advertising and consent services

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads, including banner, native, interstitial, rewarded, and app open ads. The App also uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) where required to request advertising consent.

These Google services may collect or process information such as:

  • advertising identifiers, such as the Google Advertising ID,
  • IP address, approximate location derived from IP, device model, OS version, language, and other device information,
  • ad interaction data, such as impressions, clicks, and rewarded ad completions,
  • consent status and privacy choices.

This information is processed by Google and its partners in accordance with their own privacy terms and applicable law.

1.3 Permissions the App may request

  • Internet: used to load ads and consent messages.
  • Post Notifications (Android 13+): used only if you enable reminder notifications.

The App does not request access to your contacts, photos, videos, files, microphone, or precise location for its current functionality.

2. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • to provide the speaker cleaning, speaker test, history, and reminder features,
  • to remember your in-app preferences on your device,
  • to display and manage ads,
  • to comply with legal requirements, including privacy and consent requirements where applicable,
  • to help protect advertising services against fraud, abuse, and invalid traffic.

4. Notifications and Reminders

The App may let you enable a local reminder to use the cleaning feature at a time you choose. Reminder settings are stored locally on your device. If you enable reminders, the App may send a local notification through Android's notification system.

You can disable reminders at any time within the App or through your device settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share or allow data to be processed only in the following limited ways:

  • with service providers such as Google AdMob and Google UMP,
  • when required by law, regulation, legal process, or valid governmental request,
  • to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the App or its users.

6. Data Retention

Local settings, reminder preferences, and local run history remain on your device until you clear them in the App, clear app data, or uninstall the App.

Data processed by Google AdMob or Google UMP is retained according to Google's own policies and retention practices.

7. Security

We use reasonable measures to reduce unnecessary data exposure. The App's speaker cleaning, tone generation, reminder scheduling, and local history features operate on-device. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children through our own backend because the App does not require account registration. If you believe a child has provided personal data through a third-party service connected to the App, please contact us.
