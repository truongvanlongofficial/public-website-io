Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at ForceLTE 4G/5G ("App", "we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with Google Play Developer Policies. It applies to all information collected through our mobile application, and/or any related services.

Core Principle: Our application functions primarily as an offline network diagnostic tool. We do not collect, harvest, store, or transmit your personal data, location data, or device identifiers to any of our servers.

1. Android Permissions We Request & Why

To provide network diagnostic features (such as measuring 5G/4G signal strength and cell tower metrics), the App requires specific Android OS permissions. Below is the explicit list of permissions requested and their purpose:

  • Location Permissions (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION & ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):
    Why we need it: The Android Operating System requires Location permissions to access Telephony APIs (such as reading Cell ID, EARFCN, PCI, TAC, and Signal Strength/dBm).
    How it is used: This data is strictly used in real-time to display the Telephony Dashboard on your screen.
    Data Handling: We DO NOT track your physical location. We DO NOT send your location data or cell tower data to any external servers. The data remains entirely local on your device.
  • Phone State Permission (READ_PHONE_STATE):
    Why we need it: Required to read SIM card information (such as Carrier Name, MCC, MNC, and SIM status) to show dual-SIM telemetry.
    Data Handling: We do not read your phone numbers, call logs, or contacts. This data is only used locally to identify network operators.
  • Notification Permission (POST_NOTIFICATIONS):
    Why we need it: Used to display a persistent background notification (Foreground Service) so the app can continue monitoring your signal strength (dBm) without being killed by the Android system.
  • Internet & Network State (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE, ACCESS_WIFI_STATE):
    Why we need it: Used for the in-app Ping & DNS testing tools (Network Diagnostics) and to serve advertisements via Google AdMob.
