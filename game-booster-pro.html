Privacy Policy for Game Booster

Last updated: November 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Game Booster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 VpnService Usage

Our App uses the Android VpnService class. This is a core feature of the App required to:

  • Optimize Network Latency: We use the VPN interface to prioritize gaming traffic and reduce ping.
  • DNS Routing: We may route your DNS requests to faster, public DNS servers (e.g., Google DNS, Cloudflare) to improve connection speed.

Important:

  • No Traffic Logging: We do not collect, store, or log any of your network traffic, browsing history, or data transmitted through the VPN tunnel.
  • Local Processing: All network optimization happens locally on your device. The VPN service does not route your traffic through a remote server owned by us.

1.2 Device Information

We access specific device information to provide the "Game Booster" functionality. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers:

  • Battery Status: We access battery level and charging status to display battery usage metrics.
  • CPU Temperature: We access system thermal sensors to monitor and display device temperature.
  • Network Latency (Ping): We ping public servers (e.g., 8.8.8.8) to measure and display your network stability.
  • Installed Packages: We check installed apps to identify games for optimization.

2. Permissions

We request the following permissions to provide core features:

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE & FOREGROUND_SERVICE_SPECIAL_USE: To keep the optimization service running while you play games, ensuring consistent performance.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES: To scan your device for installed games so you can add them to the booster list.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To show the active status of the booster and performance metrics in the notification shade.
  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To measure network latency (ping) and check connection status.
  • WAKE_LOCK: To prevent the device from sleeping while the game booster is active, ensuring uninterrupted gaming.
