Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Game Booster Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you use our mobile application (the "App") and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, address, or phone number directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device to improve app performance and serve relevant ads.

A. Device and Other IDs (Collected & Shared)

Our app integrates Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These services automatically collect identifiers such as:

  • Advertising ID (e.g., Google AAID)
  • Device ID
  • IP Address (Used for general location inference, not precise GPS)

Purpose:

  • Advertising: To serve personalized ads that are relevant to your interests.
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent invalid clicks or bot activity.
  • Analytics: To analyze user demographics and engagement.

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor the stability of Game Booster Pro. This tool collects:

  • Crash Logs: Stack traces regarding what caused the app to crash.
  • Diagnostics: Information about your device model, OS version, free storage, and battery level at the time of a crash.
  • System Resources Monitoring: The App monitors system resources such as RAM usage, CPU temperature, and battery health locally to provide real-time performance stats and trigger optimization tasks.

Purpose: To identify bugs, fix crashes, and ensure the app runs smoothly on your device.

C. App Activity (Collected)

We collect data on how you interact with our App, including:

  • App Interactions: Boost mode usage, game launch frequency, and screen views.

Purpose: To understand which features are most popular and to optimize the user experience.

D. Installed Applications Data (Processed Ephemerally)

Our App accesses the list of installed applications on your device to provide core gaming optimization features.

  • Purpose: To identify installed games for the Game Launcher and to detect resource-heavy background processes. This allows the App to perform memory cleanup (RAM Boosting) to ensure a maximum available RAM for your gaming session.
  • Handling: This data is processed locally on your device and is processed ephemerally. We do not store this data on our servers nor share your list of installed apps with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

4. Data Security

All data collected by our third-party partners is encrypted in transit using secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS). We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices & Opt-Out

You can opt-out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Tap "Reset advertising ID" or "Delete advertising ID".

Deleting the Advertising ID will not stop you from seeing ads, but they will be less relevant to you.

8. VpnService Usage

Our App utilizes the Android VpnService to provide network optimization features, such as the "Network Stabilizer" and "Ping Protection."

  • Functionality: The VpnService creates a local VPN tunnel to optimize game-related data packets and reduce latency (ping). It is also used to prevent background applications from consuming bandwidth while you are gaming.
  • Data Privacy: The VPN remains strictly on your device. We do not collect, log, or store any personal data transmitted through the VPN tunnel. We do not redirect your internet traffic to a remote server for monitoring or data mining.
  • Consent: The VPN is only activated when you explicitly start the "Boost" mode, and you may disconnect it at any time.
