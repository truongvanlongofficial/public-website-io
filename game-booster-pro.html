Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Game Booster ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use our mobile application (the "App"), how that information is used, and the choices available to you.

2. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, postal address, or phone number. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect technical information associated with your device.

A. Device Identifiers and Analytics Data

The App uses Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may collect information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • App instance and device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Crash logs and diagnostic data
  • App activity and event data

Purpose:

  • To measure app usage and feature engagement
  • To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
  • To show ads through Google AdMob
  • To help detect abuse, fraud, or invalid traffic

B. Installed Applications Data

The App may access the list of installed applications on your device to show games in the launcher and help organize app-specific tools inside the App.

  • Handling: This information is processed locally on your device.
  • Sharing: We do not sell your installed app list and do not use it for third-party marketing.

C. Device Status Information

The App reads certain device status information locally, such as memory usage, device temperature, battery state, and network measurements, in order to display monitoring information inside the App.

  • Handling: This information is primarily used on-device for display and app functionality.
  • Diagnostics: Limited related diagnostic information may be included in crash or analytics reports collected by Firebase services.

3. VpnService Usage

The App may use Android VpnService to enable optional on-device network tools after user permission is granted.

  • Local operation: The App does not operate a consumer VPN service and does not route your traffic through our remote servers.
  • Consent: VpnService is only activated after you explicitly allow the Android connection request.
  • Control: You can stop or revoke this permission through the App or Android system settings at any time.
  • Traffic handling: We do not state that the App guarantees lower ping, reduced lag, or improved gaming performance for every device, network, or game.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

5. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect information handled by the App and by supported third-party services. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

  • You may revoke VPN permission through Android settings at any time.
  • You may reset or delete your advertising ID in Android settings to limit ad personalization.
  • You may stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the support information provided for the App.
