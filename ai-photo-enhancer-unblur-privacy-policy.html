Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AI Photo Enhancer Unblur ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Images: Photos you select for enhancement are processed locally on your device or sent to our secure API servers for AI processing. We do NOT store your images permanently.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Premium, Google Play processes your payment. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but NOT your payment details.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, crash reports, performance data
  • Advertising ID: For showing personalized ads (free users only)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Image Processing: To enhance and upscale your photos using AI technology
  • App Functionality: To provide core features, manage subscriptions, and improve user experience
  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns and improve performance
  • Advertising: To show relevant ads to free users (Premium users see no ads)
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

3. Image Processing & Storage

Important: Your photos are processed temporarily and are NOT permanently stored on our servers.

  • Images are sent to our API server for AI processing
  • Processing typically takes 30-120 seconds
  • Images are automatically deleted from our servers after processing
  • Enhanced images are saved only to YOUR device's gallery (with your permission)

4.3 AI Processing Services

  • Purpose: Image enhancement and upscaling
  • Data Shared: Images for processing (temporary)
