Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Super Hi-Res Music Player ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Super Hi-Res Music Player is a music player application that allows you to play local audio files and stream music from online sources. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Favorites and Playlists: Songs you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Settings such as equalizer preferences, theme selection, repeat mode, and shuffle preferences are stored locally.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Audio Files: With your permission, we access audio files stored on your device to display them in the app's music library.
  • Network State: We check your network connectivity to determine whether online music streaming is available.
  • Audio Recording: We request microphone permission solely for the visualizer feature, which displays audio waveforms in real-time. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

2.3 Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Deezer API: We use Deezer's public API to stream music previews. When you use online music features, your requests are sent to Deezer's servers. Please refer to Deezer's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.
  • Google AdMob: We display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core music playback functionality
  • To display your local music library
  • To stream online music previews from Deezer
  • To save your preferences (favorites, equalizer settings, theme)
  • To display audio visualizations
  • To show relevant advertisements via AdMob
  • To improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user data (favorites, preferences, settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • No User Accounts: Our app does not require user registration or account creation.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.
  • Third-Party Security: When using online features (Deezer streaming), data is transmitted directly to Deezer's servers over secure HTTPS connections.

5. Permissions Explained

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media Access (READ_MEDIA_AUDIO, READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required to access and display your local music files.
  • Internet (INTERNET): Required to stream online music from Deezer and display advertisements.
  • Network State (ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE): Required to check if internet connection is available before attempting to stream online music.
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): Required solely for the audio visualizer feature. No audio is recorded or transmitted.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required to display playback controls in the notification area.
  • Foreground Service (FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK): Required to continue playing music when the app is in the background.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Deezer

When you use the online music streaming feature, the app connects to Deezer's API to retrieve and stream music previews. This connection is made directly from your device to Deezer's servers. We do not store or process this data.

Deezer's Privacy Policy: https://www.deezer.com/legal/personal-datas

6.2 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may use device identifiers and collect usage data to personalize ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Google's Ad Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access to Local Data: All your data (favorites, preferences) is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
  • Permissions: You can revoke any permissions granted to the app through your device settings at any time.
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings.

9. Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, it remains on your device until you:

  • Clear the app's data through device settings
  • Uninstall the application
  • Manually delete favorites or reset preferences within the app

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

11. International Users

Our app can be used worldwide. If you are using the app from outside your country, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our third-party service providers (Deezer, Google) operate.
