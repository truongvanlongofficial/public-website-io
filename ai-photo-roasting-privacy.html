Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 5, 2025

Welcome to AI-Powered Photo Roasting! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Photos and Images

AI-Powered Photo Roasting allows you to:

  • Take photos using your device's camera
  • Select photos from your device's gallery

These photos are used solely to generate AI-powered roasts (humorous commentary) about the images.

1.2 AI Analysis Data

When you submit a photo for roasting:

  • The image is sent to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis
  • The AI generates a humorous "roast" commentary about the image
  • This is a temporary processing step - images are not permanently stored by Google

1.3 Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your roast history (photos and AI-generated roasts)
  • App settings and preferences
  • User-provided API key (if you choose to use your own Google AI Studio API key)
Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your photos or roast history on any remote servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating AI roasts: Processing your photos through Google's Gemini AI to create humorous commentary
  • Displaying history: Showing your past roasts within the app
  • App functionality: Enabling features like leaderboard and history management

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Google Gemini AI

To generate roasts, we use Google's Gemini AI API:

  • Photos are temporarily sent to Google for AI analysis
  • Google's AI processes the image and returns text commentary
  • Google's data usage is governed by their Privacy Policy

3.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share your data with any other third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.

Your Control: You can optionally provide your own Google AI Studio API key in Settings. This gives you direct control over the AI service used.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage Only

All your data is stored locally using:

  • Room Database: For roast history and structured data
  • Android Preferences: For app settings
  • Device Cache: For temporary image files

4.2 Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

  • Data stored locally is protected by your device's security features
  • API keys (if provided) are stored securely using Android's encrypted preferences
  • No data transmission to our servers (we don't have any!)

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual roasts: Remove specific items from your history
  • Clear all data: Delete your entire roast history at any time
  • Uninstall the app: All local data is removed when you uninstall AI-Powered Photo Roasting
  • Camera permissions: Grant or revoke camera access in your device settings
  • Gallery access: Control photo library permissions in device settings

6. Permissions We Request

AI-Powered Photo Roasting requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos for roasting
  • Storage/Photos: To select images from your gallery (optional)
  • Internet: To communicate with Google's Gemini AI API for generating roasts

All permissions are requested with clear explanations, and you can deny them at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

AI-Powered Photo Roasting is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification (for significant changes)

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained according to your preferences:

  • Roast history persists until you manually delete it
  • Temporary image files are cleared automatically after processing
  • All data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

10. International Users

AI-Powered Photo Roasting can be used worldwide. However, please note:

  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • AI processing is handled by Google's servers (subject to their terms)
  • No data is stored or processed by us in any specific jurisdiction
