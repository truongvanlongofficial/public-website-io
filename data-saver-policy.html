Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 29, 2025

Welcome to Data Saver Pro. Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

The Most Important Part: We NOT collect, store, or monitor your personal browsing history, destination of your network traffic, or any sensitive data. Our data saving process happens locally on your device.

1. VpnService Usage

Our app uses the Android VpnService to provide its core functionality: Data Saving and Network Optimization.

  • Purpose: The VpnService creates a local secure tunnel on your device to analyze network traffic and block unnecessary background data or compress images/data to save your mobile plan.
  • No Data Transfer: All traffic processing is performed locally. None of your network traffic is ever sent to our servers or any third-party servers.
  • Transparency: We only activate the VpnService after your explicit consent via the in-app connection request.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect any personal identification information. We may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Device Information: Model, OS version, and language settings to ensure the app works correctly.
  • Performance Stats: Anonymous data about how much data was saved to improve our optimization algorithms.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements in the app. This allows us to offer the app for free. AdMob may use device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) to:

  • Show personalized ads to users in certain regions (with your consent).
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and certain US states will be presented with a consent form to manage their ad preferences in compliance with GDPR and US state laws.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of the data optimization process on your device.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under the GDPR (Europe) or CCPA/CPRA (California), including the right to withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time through the app settings.
