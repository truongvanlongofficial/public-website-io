Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Welcome to Battery Health Pro. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Transparency Note: We are committed to honesty and transparency. We do not sell your personal data. Most of our features operate locally on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

To provide a functional and optimized experience, we may collect the following types of information:

A. Battery & Device Information

The app accesses system information related to your battery status, including temperature, voltage, health status, and capacity. This data is used solely to display real-time information to you and to perform battery optimization tasks.

B. Location Data (Sensitive Permission)

Our app includes a "Charging History Map" feature. To provide this, we request access to your device's location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION).

  • Purpose: To record and display the physical location where your phone was plugged in for charging.
  • Storage: This location data is stored locally on your device only (using private app storage).
  • Privacy: We DO NOT upload your location data to our servers or share it with any third parties.

C. Application Management

The "Optimization" feature scans for background processes to help save battery. It identifies running applications to allow you to terminate background tasks. We do not collect or monitor your personal data within those other applications.

2. Permissions We Request

  • Location: Used for the charging history map (stored locally).
  • Notifications: Used for the Anti-theft alarm and battery status alerts.
  • Foreground Service: Required to keep the Anti-theft alarm active even when the app is in the background.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These include:

  • Google Play Services: Required for app functionality and updates.
  • Google AdMob: We show advertisements to keep the app free. AdMob may collect your Advertising ID and device identifiers to provide personalized ads. You can manage your ad preferences in your Google Account settings.

4. Log Data

In case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
