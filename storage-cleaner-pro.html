Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Welcome to Storage Analyzer Pro ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Core Functionality and "All Files Access" (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

Storage Analyzer Pro is a dedicated storage management and junk cleaning tool. To provide its core functionality, the App requires the "All Files Access" (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE) permission on Android 11+ and Read/Write External Storage permissions on older versions.

Why do we need this permission?

  • To comprehensively scan your device's storage for unnecessary files, cache, empty folders, and large media files.
  • To identify duplicate files and screenshots across multiple directories (including internal storage and SD card).
  • To allow you to safely delete these junk files to free up storage space.

How we use it: This permission is used strictly locally on your device. We do not copy, upload, transfer, or share your personal files, photos, videos, or documents with our servers or any third parties. The scanning and deletion processes happen entirely offline within your device.

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, language settings, and device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID).
