Internal TikTok Content Management Tool

This website represents an internal web-based tool used by the developer to manage and publish video content to TikTok accounts owned and operated by the developer.

1. Connect to TikTok

The app connects to TikTok through official authorization in order to publish video content to the developer's own accounts.

✔ TikTok authorization successful

2. Upload & Publish Video

After authorization, the developer can upload a video and publish it directly to TikTok.


✔ Video published successfully
This tool is for internal use only and is not a public SaaS product.
